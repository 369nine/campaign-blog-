THE COMMUNITY

In a world filled with cruelty, and little to no kindness; a mean atmosphere of oppression and discrimination. Brace yourself, for the time of innovation has finally come. We, as the soul city community, are here to bring change. Believe it or not, we have found our true mission in this world . . . and that is to awaken the sleeping. We believe that the only way we can change the world is by changing ourselves. Inorder to change yourself, the first thing you need to do is to change the way you see yourself. Who you think you are is the most powerful will given to humanity by the powerful sources of life.

With all that said, we started an open community of people with the same goal. Amongst us are people from different regions in this world, brought together to join heads and figure out a way to move forward. Moving forward, in our understanding, is creating change. As cliche as it may sound, that is what we truly want.

THE TARGET AUDIENCE

Our goal is to recruit as many young people as possible. And the reason behind that is our core belief that the future lies with the upcoming generation. I am not necessarily saying that the older generation is not allowed to join this innovation, but our MAIN audience is infact the youth.
